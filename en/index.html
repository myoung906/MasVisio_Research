Mission

Our mission is very clear - provide best and independent research at the highest standard in vision rehabilitation. As an independent institute, our studies are conducted impartially and unprejudiced. This leads to higher credibility of the results, which is crucial for our customers and the advancement of vision rehabilitation technology.

MasVisio Research was founded with the vision of bridging the gap between cutting-edge vision rehabilitation research and clinical applications. We specialize in neural rehabilitation technologies, advanced visual prosthetics, and personalized rehabilitation protocols. Our interdisciplinary approach combines neuroscience, bioengineering, optical technology, and rehabilitation medicine to develop innovative solutions for individuals with visual impairments and low vision conditions.

🔬 Clinical Trials & Clinical Tests
Biomedical research studies on human participants designed to answer specific questions about vision rehabilitation therapies, neural stimulation protocols, and assistive device efficacy. All studies are conducted following ICH-GCP guidelines and ethical committee approvals.
⚙️ Laboratory Tests & Studies
Comprehensive testing of visual prosthetic devices, neural interface materials, and rehabilitation equipment performance under various conditions. Our modern laboratories in Seoul provide precise measurements and quality assessment for device development.
🎯 Device Development
Enhancement and advancement of medical devices for vision rehabilitation, including neural stimulation systems, adaptive visual aids, and AI-powered assistive technologies. Focus on practical implementation and clinical applicability.
📊 Materials Testing
Measurement of characteristics and behavior of biocompatible materials, neural interface components, and optical materials under physiological conditions. Ensuring safety and longevity of implantable devices.
📈 Market Research
Gathering and evaluation of data regarding patients' preferences for rehabilitation technologies and services. Understanding market needs for vision rehabilitation solutions and assistive device adoption patterns.
👥 International Expertise
Our research group consists of international experts with extensive specialist knowledge in vision rehabilitation, neural engineering, and clinical research. Regular collaboration with global research institutions ensures cutting-edge methodologies.
