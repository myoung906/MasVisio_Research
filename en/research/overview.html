Pan-retinal Full Correction & Myopia Control

Pan-retinal Full Correction and Progressive Myopia Control

We study optical intervention methods to suppress myopia progression by analyzing the relationship between peripheral retinal focal plane position and eye growth. We investigate myopia suppression mechanisms through controlling FS' focal line position of oblique rays.

Biomarkers in Ocular Matrices

Integrated Biometrics-based Ocular Disease Prediction

We simultaneously evaluate diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma, mild cognitive impairment, and psychological traits using 47 biomarkers that integrate pupil dynamics and contrast sensitivity. This is the world's first non-invasive integrated screening platform targeting over 90% accuracy with a 3-minute examination.

A Real-time Autonomous Refractometer by Supervised Learning

Retinoscopy-based Real-time Auto Refractometer

We analyze the thickness, brightness, and speed of reflected rays by diopter using supervised learning to return probabilistic refractive error amounts. This is a real-time auto refractometer that targets real-time measurement within 5 seconds regardless of examiner skill level.
