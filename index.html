Our Approach

Three Research Pillars

PX-01 Precision Optics Measurement

Capturing micro-level precision data, from 3D corneo-scleral shapes to minute pupil tremors, using Infrared Structured Light and high-speed ROI tracking systems.

PX-02 AI Biomarker Analysis

Analyzing collected visual data with proprietary deep learning models to detect early signs of degenerative brain diseases and ocular pathologies as non-invasive digital biomarkers.

PX-03 Personalized Vision Solutions

Providing high-fidelity myopia progression models and personalized vision rehabilitation designs by integrating individual ocular optical spectra and lifestyle data.

What We Study

Research Areas

Myopia Control Modeling

Researching 4D peripheral optical pathways using ultra-precision ray tracing models that simulate refraction boundaries across 6 ocular surfaces.

AI-Driven Ocular Screening

Developing non-invasive AI screening networks that detect early autonomic abnormalities by evaluating pupil dynamics and high-dimensional biometric indices.

Corneo-Scleral 3D Topology

Building custom scleral fitting profiles by mapping deformed structured light concentric ring vectors to reconstruct 3D topologies in real-time.
