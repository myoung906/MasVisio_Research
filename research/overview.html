Research Challenges

Pioneering solutions for next-generation vision diagnostics and rehabilitation.

Motion Contrast Sensitivity in Visual Pathways

Investigating the correlation between visual pathway dominance and motion contrast sensitivity. We analyze detection thresholds for stationary and moving stimuli in simulated hemianopsia conditions.

Key Focus: Validating visual pathway dominance through hemianopsia simulation and contrast sensitivity analysis.

View Project Details →

Corneo-Scleral Profiling &
3D Reconstruction

A novel system for full-range anterior segment profiling. By reconstructing the 3D topography of both the cornea and sclera, we enable precise fitting of scleral lenses and advanced myopia control interventions.

Key Focus: 3D modeling of the ocular surface for personalized contact lens design.

Retinal Profiling &
3D Reconstruction

Utilizing OCT and fundus imaging data to generate comprehensive 3D retinal maps. This technology allows for the precise tracking of retinal layer thickness changes, crucial for monitoring myopia progression and macular degeneration.

Key Focus: Quantitative analysis of retinal curvature and peripheral defocus.

Real-time Auto Refractometer

Next-generation wavefront sensing technology that provides continuous, real-time refractive error measurements. This system is designed for dynamic accommodation studies and pediatric vision screening where speed and accuracy are paramount.

Key Focus: Binocular open-field autorefraction with high temporal resolution.

Non-invasive General Disorder
Prediction Algorithm

An AI-driven platform that correlates ocular biomarkers with systemic health issues. By analyzing micro-vascular changes in the retina and anterior segment, our algorithm predicts risks for diabetes, hypertension, and cardiovascular diseases non-invasively.

Key Focus: Multi-modal risk assessment using standard ocular imaging.
