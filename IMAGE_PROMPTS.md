# DALL-E 3 이미지 생성 프롬프트 모음

이 문서는 MasVisio Research 웹사이트의 핵심 시각 자료를 생성하기 위한 DALL-E 3 프롬프트 모음입니다.
모든 이미지는 **"Premium, Futuristic, Medical, Trustworthy"** 톤앤매너를 유지합니다.

## 1. 핵심 연구 성과 (Key Research Achievements)

### 1-1. 임상 성공률 95.2% (Clinical Success Rate)
**목표**: 95.2%라는 압도적인 성공률을 직관적이고 신뢰감 있게 표현.
**스타일**: 3D Glassmorphism, Clean, High-tech Medical Blue.
**프롬프트 (English)**:
> A high-quality, futuristic 3D data visualization of a medical clinical trial success rate. A glowing crystal clear circular progress bar showing "95.2%" filled with a vibrant cyan and pharmaceutical blue gradient. The background is a deep, sleek dark blue laboratory interface with subtle holographic data particles floating. The text "95.2%" is elegant, modern, and glowing. The overall atmosphere is professional, cutting-edge, and trustworthy. Cinematic lighting, 8k resolution, highly detailed.

### 1-2. 바이오마커 기반 AI 진단 (AI Diagnostic Platform)
**목표**: 47개의 바이오마커와 AI의 연결성을 시각화.
**스타일**: Neural Network, Connected, Digital Twin.
**프롬프트 (English)**:
> A sophisticated artistic representation of an AI-powered eye diagnostic system. A detailed wireframe model of a human eye in the center, connected to a glowing neural network of nodes representing 47 different biomarkers. The nodes are connected by pulses of light. The aesthetic is deep tech, using shades of emerald green and electric blue. Glassmorphism effects on the interface elements. Abstract, scientific, and precise. 8k, unreal engine 5 render style.

### 1-3. 질환별 진단 성능 (Diagnostic Performance)
**목표**: 다양한 안구 질환에 대한 높은 진단 정확도를 비교 표현.
**스타일**: Bar Chart, Holographic, Comparative.
**프롬프트 (English)**:
> A futuristic holographic bar chart displaying diagnostic accuracy for different eye diseases. The bars are made of translucent glass with internal glowing light. The highest bars reach near 100%. Labels are subtle and futuristic. The chart floats above a sleek, reflective black surface. Cool color palette with neon accents to highlight key data points. Professional medical data presentation.
